Amardaha Town (अमरदह बजार)

A small town in eastern Nepal, situated at 26.5282° N, 87.5495° E

Brief Info

Time zone: +5:45(UTC), Nepal uses NPT as time zone abbriviation around the year.

Official name: Amardaha

It used to have its own local goverment body called VDC (village develoment commitee) but a recent restructure from central goverment made it part of a new municipality - Sunbarshi Nunicipality (सुनबर्शी नगरपालिका)
